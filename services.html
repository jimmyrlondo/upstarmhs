Our Services

Full-Spectrum Mental Health & Behavioral Support

From individual therapy to community case management, Upstar offers a comprehensive range of services designed to meet each client exactly where they are and help them get where they want to go.

01
Therapy

Individual & Family Group Therapy

Our team of qualified therapists meets with clients to understand their requirements and establish goals aimed at reducing or enhancing mental health symptoms. The most effective therapeutic modality is determined by the therapist, who then develops a personalized plan.

Services are delivered through individual therapy sessions, group therapy sessions, or interactions with the complete family or support network to best support your healing journey.

02
SUD

Chemical Dependency Counseling & SUD Case Management

Our substance use disorder (SUD) counseling and case management services provide structured, evidence-based support for individuals navigating chemical dependency and recovery. We address both the clinical and practical dimensions of recovery helping clients build lasting wellness.

03
Case Management

Case Management Services

Our qualified behavioral health specialists engage with clients in a community environment to enhance their personal and social functioning. We give our clients the tools and resources they need to live more productive, independent lives via advocacy and support.

Case managers work together with the client and therapist to create a thorough treatment plan that addresses all requirements and goals. Services include:

  • Client Referral
  • Housing Application Assistance
  • Clothing Vouchers
  • Disability Applications
  • Driver's License (obtaining/reinstating)
  • Education Support
  • Coping Behavior Skills
  • Anger Management Assistance
  • Depression Coping Skills
  • Career Planning & Job Skills
  • Medicaid Application Assistance
  • Physician Referral
  • Social Security Applications
  • Community Resource Connections
04
OhioRISE

OhioRISE Services

OhioRISE (Resilience through Integrated Systems and Excellence) is Ohio's specialized high-intensity care coordination program for youth with the most complex behavioral and mental health needs. Upstar is an authorized OhioRISE provider, delivering intensive, coordinated care to help youth and families access the full range of services they need.

05
Youth

School-Based & Youth Services

Upstar offers classroom interventions and in-school counseling to help kids and teenagers behave better in class and deal with personal or in-school stressors.

Services are tailored to each student and may include:

  • Individual Counseling (at school or home)
  • Group Counseling Sessions
  • Prevention Groups
  • Anger Control Programs
  • Violence Avoidance Programs
  • Social Skills Development

Common diagnoses we support:

ADHD Mood Disorders Behavior Disorders Anxiety Adjustment Reactions Post-Traumatic Stress (PTSD) Depression
06
CPST

Therapeutic Behavioral Services & CPST

Community Psychiatric Supportive Treatment (CPST) and Therapeutic Behavioral Services provide structured, community-based psychiatric support to help clients develop skills, stabilize symptoms, and function effectively in daily life.

These services are designed for individuals who need more support than outpatient therapy alone delivered right in the community where our clients live and work.

07
Peer Support

Peer Support Services

Our lived-experience peer support specialists walk alongside clients on their path to recovery. Peer support offers a uniquely powerful connection with someone who truly understands the challenges our clients face.

Peer support bridges the gap between clinical care and real-world recovery, offering hope, connection, and practical guidance.
