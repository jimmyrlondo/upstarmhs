FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about getting started and our services.

You can start by completing our online intake form on the "Start Services" page, or by emailing us at info@upstarmhs.com. Our team will reach out to schedule an initial assessment and discuss which services are the best fit.

Yes. We are committed to serving underserved individuals and accept Ohio Medicaid. Our team can help you with Medicaid applications if needed as part of our case management services.

We serve clients of all ages from young children to adults. Our school-based and youth engagement services are specifically designed for children and teenagers, while our therapy and case management services support adults as well.

OhioRISE is a specialized program for Ohio youth (under 21) with complex behavioral and mental health needs who are on Medicaid. If your child has significant challenges managing daily life, school, or relationships due to behavioral health conditions, they may qualify. Contact us and we can help assess eligibility.

We provide both office-based and community-based services. Many of our case management, CPST, and peer support services are delivered directly in clients' homes, schools, and communities so you receive support wherever you need it most.

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, please call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) immediately, or call 911. Upstar does not provide emergency crisis services, but we can help connect you with ongoing support after stabilization.
