About Us

Compassionate Care for Every Community

Upstar Mental Health Services is committed to advancing overall wellness for youth and adults across the state of Ohio through advocacy, support, and personalized care.

Our Mission

Advancing Overall Wellness Through Community Mental Health

Through community mental health services, our goal is to advance overall wellness. Upstar cares for people, adolescents, children, and their families with compassion and support.

We focus on providing outpatient and community mental healthcare to underserved individuals and children giving our clients the tools and resources they need to live more productive, independent lives via advocacy and support.

Our team of qualified therapists and behavioral health specialists work collaboratively with each client to create thorough, individualized treatment plans that address their unique requirements and goals.

Compassion First

Every client is treated with dignity, respect, and genuine care at every touchpoint of their journey.

Community-Centered

We meet clients where they are in their homes, schools, and communities for real-world impact.

Evidence-Based Care

Our qualified clinicians use proven therapeutic modalities tailored to each client's individual needs.

Advocacy & Empowerment

We advocate for our clients' rights and access to resources that support independent, productive lives.

Who We Serve

Meeting Every Client Where They Are

We serve individuals across the lifespan from young children navigating school to adults facing complex behavioral health challenges.

Children (All Ages)

Behavioral support, school-based counseling, and prevention programs to help children build healthy coping skills and thrive.

Adolescents & Teens

Specialized support for teenagers dealing with anxiety, depression, ADHD, behavioral challenges, and social stressors.

Adults

Individual therapy, SUD counseling, community psychiatric support, and case management for adults of all backgrounds.

Families

Family therapy sessions that strengthen the entire support network and create lasting change for everyone involved.
