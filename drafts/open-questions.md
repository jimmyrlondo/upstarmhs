# Open Questions for Leadership Confirmation

| Decision needed | Recommended default | Impact if deferred |
|---|---|---|
| Final policy effective date and rollout sequence | Approve manual within 30-60 days (including board/legal review steps), then phase implementation over 60-90 days | Inconsistent adoption across programs |
| Which governance model applies (formal board governance standards vs leadership-only model) | Apply governance practices in this draft; formal board governance review quarterly | Unclear accountability boundaries |
| Ohio legal citation set to embed in compliance section | Keep placeholders and complete legal review before final issuance | Compliance ambiguity for audits/contracts |
| Standard timeframe for access-to-care targets (first contact to intake/first appointment) | Set initial benchmarks by service line and review monthly | Variable access performance and equity blind spots |
| Required follow-up window after discharge/transition | Start with outreach within 7 calendar days for routine discharges and within 48 hours for high-risk discharges (e.g., recent crisis event, active safety concerns, or complex care handoff risk) | Uneven continuity-of-care practices and delayed risk response |
| Scope of medication services by program (control/administer/prescribe) | Document scope per program and apply medication policy only where applicable | Risk of over- or under-applying medication controls |
| Restrictive intervention policy applicability by program | Default to de-escalation-only; allow restraint/seclusion only where legally permitted and trained | Safety and legal risk if scope is unclear |
| Telehealth modality boundaries (audio-only eligibility, platform standards) | Permit audio/video based on clinical appropriateness and payer/legal requirements; maintain approved platform list | Billing/compliance variability and inconsistent care quality |
| Records retention schedule by record type | Adopt interim schedule aligned to payer/licensure minimums pending legal confirmation | Inconsistent destruction/retention decisions |
| KPI set for annual performance plan | Launch with minimum set: outcomes, experience, access, safety, workforce, finance | Delays in performance-based decision making |
