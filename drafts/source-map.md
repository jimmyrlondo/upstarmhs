## Legend
- **Manual Section** — the policy in the Upstar manual.

- **Description** — the substance of the requirement addressed.
- **Notes** — conditional applicability, synthesis of multiple standards, optional standards, and any standards intentionally deferred or marked Not Applicable.

## Source Notes
- The CARF source text (`carf/carf.txt`) contains interleaved page markers and repeated index/heading lines across printed-page boundaries; the detailed Section 1 standards begin near line 2188 and Section 2 standards near line 10158 of the extracted text.
- Standard numbers cited reflect the 2026 Manual numbering. Several 2026 relocations are noted in-manual (e.g., technology/data-use governance consolidated under 1.G/1.A).
- Upstar manual language operationalizes the standards (who/what/when/how) rather than quoting CARF verbatim. Conditional standards are implemented with explicit applicability statements so auditors can confirm scope.
