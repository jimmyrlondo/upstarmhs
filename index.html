Our Mission

Advancing Overall Wellness Through Community

Through community mental health services, our goal is to advance overall wellness. Upstar cares for people, adolescents, children, and their families with compassion and support and provide outpatient services that meet clients where they are.

Who We Serve

Support for Youth, Families & Adults

We serve youth and adults of all ages across Ohio, from children navigating school challenges to adults managing complex behavioral health needs.

Children & Youth

School-based counseling, behavioral support, ADHD, anxiety, mood disorders, and prevention programs for kids and teens.

Families

Family therapy sessions and care coordination that supports the entire support network, not just the individual.

Adults & Individuals

Individual therapy, SUD counseling, CPST services, and case management for adults navigating life's challenges.

Underserved Communities

Dedicated to reaching those who lack access to quality mental healthcare with Medicaid-accepted services and community outreach.
